Ophthalmogenetics

At Watany Eye Hospitals, we are proud to introduce a new service the ‘Ophthalmogenetics Clinic’. The clinic will be taking care of patients with genetic eye diseases with special emphasis on inherited retinal dystrophies and hereditary optic neuropathies. The service will include diagnosis, management, genetic testing, as well as genetic counseling of inherited ophthalmological pathologies
