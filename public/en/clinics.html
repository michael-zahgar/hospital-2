Cataract

Cataract clinics provide a variety of services related to the diagnosis, and treatment of cataracts. All types of lenses are implanted, including soft lenses, astigmatism-correcting lenses, and multifocal lenses that replace the use of reading glasses.

Refractive

In this clinic, the finest equipment and modern technology are used, refractive defects are corrected using femto-LASIK rays, excimer lasers, or lens implants to treat these defects.

See More

Medical Retina

At the medical retina clinic, we specialize at providing treatment to patients with diagnosed retinal diseases. Management options include systemic or topical treatment, laser therapy, as well as intravitreal injections.

See More

03

Vitreoretinal Surgery

The vitreoretinal clinic has the latest and most advanced diagnostic equipment in the world, providing a comprehensive range of surgical and medical treatments for retinal and vitreous diseases.

See More

04

Glaucoma

The glaucoma clinic offers a variety of services to diagnose and treat glaucoma, a condition that damages the optic nerve and can lead to vision loss.

See More

05

Oculopasty

Oculoplastic surgery includes a wide variety of surgical procedures that deal with the orbit (eye socket), eyelids, tear ducts, and the face.

See More

06

Cornea and External Eye Diseases

Cornea and external eye diseases clinics offer a wide range of services to diagnose and treat conditions that affect the front of the eye.

See More

07

Uveitis

This clinic is one of the essential specialized clinics at the hospital. In this clinic, diseases of the iris, ciliary body, choroid, and infections of the eye layers caused by immune disorders are treated.

See More

08

Neuro-ophthalmology

Watany Eye Hospitals provide specialized care for neuro-ophthalmic diseases. At our hospitals, the patient will receive a comprehensive evaluation and treatment of these diseases, many of which threaten the integrity of vision.

See More

09

Squint

Squint/Strabismus clinics provide a variety of services to diagnose and treat strabismus, a condition in which the eyes are not aligned properly.

Pediatric Eye Diseases

At our pediatric eye clinic, the children receive the finest eye care for treatment of different kinds of pediatric eye diseases.

Ocular Surface Diseases
