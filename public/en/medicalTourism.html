Hassle free experience

. Accommodation

We understand the importance of comfortable accommodation for patients and their companions. Watany Eye Hospitals collaborates with reputable hotels and resorts in the vicinity, offering a range of options to suit different preferences and budgets. These accommodations are conveniently located and provide a tranquil environment conducive to relaxation and recovery. The Hotels are JW Marriot & Renaissance in Cairo and Marriot in Hurghada. Our dedicated staff can assist in making reservations and ensuring that patients receive special rates and amenities during their stay.

. Transportation

Watany Eye Hospitals aims to make travel arrangements as convenient as possible for medical tourists. We provide assistance in arranging airport transfers, ensuring seamless transportation from the airport to the hospitals or chosen accommodation. Additionally, we offer transportation services for patients requiring post-operative follow-up visits or other related appointments. Our team coordinates these arrangements, prioritizing comfort, safety, and punctuality.

. All Services in One Place

Watany Eye Hospitals are designed to meet all your medical needs efficiently and seamlessly. With our integrated approach, you can access comprehensive eye exams, diagnostic investigations, surgical procedures, pharmacy services, laboratory tests, optics consultations, and even obtain contact lenses all within the same premises.

Doctors Professionalism

At our eye hospital, professionalism is the cornerstone of our doctors' practice. Our team of highly skilled ophthalmologists is dedicated to providing exceptional care with unwavering professionalism and compassion.

Latest technology

We continuously invest in the latest equipment and technology to ensure that our patients have access to the most advanced medical treatments available. We are also certified form the Center of Excellence for having the Latest equipment.

Suitable Prices

Flexible payment options

Years of Experience

With a legacy spanning +20 years, our eye hospital boasts a rich history of providing unparalleled care and transforming lives through vision. Throughout the decades, our commitment to excellence, innovation, and patient-centered care has remained unwavering. Over the years, we have earned the trust and gratitude of millions of patients who have entrusted us with their vision, and their continued satisfaction serves as a testament to our enduring legacy.
