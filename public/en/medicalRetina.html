Medical Retina

At the medical retina clinic, we specialize at providing treatment to patients with diagnosed retinal disease, such as diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration and retinal vein occlusion. A wide range of management options are offered to our patients, including systemic or topical treatment, laser therapy, as well as intravitreal injections.

• High blood sugar levels in uncontrolled diabetes can damage the retina extensively if left untreated.

• Blurred vision

• Sudden vision loss

• Gradual worsening of vision

• Difficulty seeing in the dark

• First, the patient must make sure that they control their blood sugar levels to avoid further damage.

• Surgery to remove blood or scar tissue from the eyes

• Direct injections and medications to the eyes.
