Medical Tourism
at Watany Eye Hospitals

Watany Eye Hospitals is a leading medical institution specializing in eye care and ophthalmic surgeries, located in a prime location that offers a perfect blend of medical expertise and hospitality. We take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities, highly skilled doctors, and a commitment to providing exceptional care to patients seeking various eye treatments, including LASIK, Femto-LASIK, and blepharoplasty. In addition to our medical services, we strive to ensure that patients and their accompanying persons have a comfortable and hassle-free experience during their stay, with convenient accommodation options, transportation facilities, and on-site amenities.

At Watany Eye Hospitals, all your eye medical needs are met under one roof to ensure that you receive the highest quality care in a seamless and efficient manner. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our investigations department utilizes cutting-edge technology to provide accurate diagnostics, while our specialized clinics cater to a variety of medical needs, from primary care to specialized treatments. With our expert surgeons and advanced operating rooms, we perform a wide range of operations with precision and care. Additionally, our contact lens center offers personalized solutions for vision correction, while our fully equipped laboratory provides timely and accurate test results. Need glasses or contact lenses? Visit our optics department for a wide selection of frames and lenses to suit your style and prescription. And to ensure you have access to the medications you need, our pharmacy is stocked with a comprehensive selection of prescription and over-the-counter drugs.

Our Hospital is equipped with the latest medical equipment to ensure precise diagnoses and effective treatments for a wide range of eye conditions. From advanced imaging systems for detailed assessments to cutting-edge laser technology for refractive surgeries, we offer our patients access to the most innovative tools available. Our experienced ophthalmologists leverage these technologies to provide personalized care, whether you're seeking routine eye exams, cataract surgery, or complex retinal procedures. We are certified with a badge from the Center of Excellence for the most advanced equipment we have.

We understand the critical need to maintain a safe and sterile environment to safeguard the health and well-being of our patients. From meticulously sanitized examination rooms to the thorough sterilization of surgical equipment, we leave no stone unturned in ensuring a clean and hygienic environment.

Our team of ophthalmologists exemplifies the pinnacle of professionalism, expertise, and empathy in the field of ophthalmology. From routine eye examinations to complex surgical procedures, our eye doctors approach each case with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring the highest standards of precision and safety. Moreover, our commitment to staying abreast of the latest advancements in ophthalmic technology and techniques underscores our dedication to delivering cutting-edge treatments with compassion and integrity. You are in the hands of true professionals who prioritize your visual health and well-being above all else.

Our journey of 23 years is a testament to our enduring passion for preserving and enhancing vision, as well as our steadfast commitment to the well-being of our patients. As we embark on the next chapter of our mission, we remain dedicated to providing comprehensive eye care services of the highest quality, ensuring that every patient receives the personalized attention and exceptional treatment they deserve.
